Community Standards

Last updated: March 20, 2026

Scone is for sharing recipes, food moments, and ideas in a respectful way. These standards explain what is and is not allowed.

What we expect

  • Be respectful and communicate in good faith.
  • Share content you have the right to post.
  • Use report and block tools when you encounter something unsafe.

Not allowed

  • Harassment, bullying, threats, intimidation, or targeted abuse.
  • Hate speech or content attacking people based on identity.
  • Sexually explicit or exploitative content.
  • Graphic violence or threats of violence.
  • Spam, scams, manipulation, or repeated unwanted contact.
  • Impersonation, doxxing, or sharing private information without permission.

Enforcement

When content or accounts break these rules, we may remove content, restrict features, or deactivate accounts. Serious or repeated violations may lead to permanent removal.

Reporting

You can report posts, comments, recipes, and profiles in the app. If you need help with a report, contact sconerecipeapp@gmail.com.
