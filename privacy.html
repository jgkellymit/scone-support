Privacy Policy

Last updated:

This Privacy Policy explains how the Scone app (“Scone”, “we”, “us”) handles information when you use the app.

Information you provide

  • Account information such as email and profile details (if you choose to provide them).
  • Content you create in the app (recipes, posts, comments, photos, and other content).
  • Support messages you send to us (including any attachments).

Information collected automatically

  • Basic diagnostics and usage information (e.g., crash logs) to help us improve reliability.
  • Device and app information such as device model, OS version, and app version.

How we use information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app.
  • Respond to support requests.
  • Keep the app safe and prevent abuse.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information with service providers that help us operate the app (for example, hosting and storage), and as required by law.

Data retention

We keep information for as long as needed to provide the service and for legitimate business purposes such as security, dispute resolution, and compliance.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us at support@sconeapp.com.
